Berries: Small, juicy fruits with seeds embedded in flesh, like blueberries and raspberries

Drupes: Fruits with a fleshy outer layer and a hard seed inside, such as peaches, plums, and cherries.

Citrus Fruits: Tangy, acidic fruits with a thick rind and juicy pulp, including oranges, lemons, limes, and grapefruits.

Tropical Fruits: Fruits that grow in warm climates and have unique flavors and textures, like mangoes, pineapples, papayas, and bananas.

Stone Fruits: Fleshy fruits with a large, hard seed or "stone" inside, such as peaches, apricots, and nectarines.

Pomes: Fruits with a central core containing seeds, surrounded by a firm flesh, like apples and pears.

Melons: Large, juicy fruits with thick rinds and sweet, watery flesh, such as watermelons cantaloupes
